About FlashCards

FlashCards was created by Christophe Lee, a developer passionate about creating tools that make learning more effective and enjoyable.

Christophe Lee

Creator & Developer

The Story Behind FlashCards

As a lifelong learner, Christophe found himself constantly struggling to retain information across various subjects. Existing learning tools either lacked flexibility or were too cumbersome for daily use.

In 2023, he decided to build FlashCards as a personal solution - a clean, intuitive app focused specifically on helping learners master information through spaced repetition. What began as a side project quickly grew into a full-fledged application used by thousands.

The Mission

To create learning tools that are simple enough for casual use but powerful enough for serious study, helping people transform information into lasting knowledge.

Built With Care

Every aspect of FlashCards reflects Christophe's attention to detail:

  • Minimalist design that keeps the focus on learning
  • Custom spaced repetition algorithm developed through testing
  • Responsive interface that works seamlessly across devices
  • Regular updates based on user feedback

Get In Touch

Christophe welcomes feedback and suggestions from all users. If you have ideas for improving FlashCards or just want to share your learning journey, please reach out!

Your experience with FlashCards helps shape its future development and ensures it continues to meet learners' needs.
